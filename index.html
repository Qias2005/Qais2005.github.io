Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is the drone delivery service?
Our drones typically deliver within 15-30 minutes depending on your location in Amman.
Is the delivery contactless?
Yes, all deliveries are fully contactless for safety and convenience.
Can I track my order?
Absolutely! Our mobile app lets you track your delivery in real-time.
What if the drone can’t reach my address?
We will notify you immediately and provide alternative options such as manual delivery or pickup points.
Are drones safe to use in urban areas?
Yes, our drones are equipped with advanced sensors and safety protocols to avoid obstacles and ensure safe flights.
What is the maximum weight a drone can carry?
Currently, our drones can carry packages weighing up to 5 kilograms, suitable for most food and essential deliveries.
How do you handle bad weather?
For safety, drone operations are paused during severe weather conditions. We notify customers promptly and reschedule deliveries if needed.
Can I schedule a delivery for a specific time?
Yes, the app will allow you to select preferred delivery windows once launched.
