About Keeta Jordan

Keeta Jordan is the leading drone delivery service based in Amman, Jordan, specializing in fast, safe, and eco-friendly drone deliveries. Whether it’s your favorite food, coffee, or essential items, our innovative technology ensures quick and reliable shipping across the city.

Our Mission

At Keeta Jordan, we aim to revolutionize delivery services in Jordan by utilizing cutting-edge drone technology to provide efficient, contactless, and environmentally friendly delivery options. Our commitment is to enhance convenience for our customers and reduce the carbon footprint of traditional delivery methods.
